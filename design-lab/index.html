A Spectrum of UX Challenges & Creative Work.

Clients

With over a decade of experience as a Senior UX and Product Designer, I have focused on building user-centered solutions and leading design initiatives for world-class brands across various industries.
My work has involved leading UX initiatives for key projects with clients such as IBM, Google, Ulta Beauty, Telus, Anheuser-Busch and more.
Across all projects, my approach is to own the end-to-end design process; from initial research and strategy to delivering scalable, high-value UX solutions that align with both user needs and business goals.

Google

Mockup of one of the Cutomer Reference forms attached to a client database.

While at IBM, I collaborated directly with Google on a custom CRM buildout using the Salesforce Lightning Design System for their Google Cloud Service. Our goal was to replace a legacy system of managing specific advertising assets with a central repository to house everything related to a client's campaign, from videos and stills to usage metrics.

I flew to their headquarters in Sunnyvale, California to work hands-on with their creative team.

As the lead UX for this initiative I met daily with product owners and senior engineers to align on business goals and user needs. My responsibility was to design the essential workflows and deliver a robust, simplified CRM to their development team for implementation.

Blue Cross Blue Shield

Main page of the bcbsla member portal.

At Blue Cross Blue Shield, I was responsible for certain areas of the online member portal. My work included maintaining the primary landing page, building out a comprehensive mega menu system, and implementing micro sites for various products and campaigns.

I ensured the portal remained intuitive, met all usability compliance standards, and adhered to design best practices.

Telus

One of the many user flows to manage customer accounts.

I was the designer responsible for a year-long CRM buildout for Telus, a major Canadian telecommunication company. Using the Salesforce Lightning Design System, the goal was to create a comprehensive solution to manage all of their diverse clients and services.

I built dozens of unique portals and record pages, all designed to form a single, scalable system for their teams.
